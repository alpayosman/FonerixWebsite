Privacy Policy

At Fonerix LTD., we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, apply for distributorship, or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal data you provide through forms (name, email, phone, company details)
  • Information collected through cookies (browser type, IP address, session behavior)
  • Business-related communications and preferences

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your distributorship applications
  • To communicate with you regarding business opportunities
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations under applicable laws

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with our trusted business partners, service providers, or legal authorities when required by law or with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement administrative and technical measures to ensure your data remains protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. These include encryption, access controls, and secure storage.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy regulations, including the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to object to its processing.

6. Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding your personal data, you may contact us using the details provided on our website.
